Ready Player One

The Movie of the Future!

An interesting and eerily predictive movie about what the future could look like for our world when virtual reality takes over. In the movie, human kind spends all of their free time, and even for some, their work and school time, logged into the Oasis - an ever expanding virtually rendered world where you can be anyone or anything, and go anywhere without any limit but for your own imagination and that of the coders who created the software.
