About Me!


Hello! My name is Nicolette, Nikki for short, and I am currently a Junior in the Web Design and Development program at Arkansas State University. I'm a non-traditional student, thirty-two years old with a full time job as a Manager at McDonalds, though I've been looking for years for something to go to school that I am passionate about, and I've finally found it in web design!


I love all aspects of design, color theory, typography, creating wire frames, though the coding is a bit harder than I expected! Despite the struggles, I am thoroughly enjoying my learning experience and am excited to graduate in the Summer of 2022 and enter the world of Web Design professionally.


Wish me luck!
